About

I am an electronic engineer interested in Physics, Astronomy and Space Technology.

My work is with the Array Maintenance Group of the Atacama Millimeter/Submillimeter Array (ALMA) Observatory. My duty is to provide maintenance solutions to the Correlators and Backend subsystems of the array, ensuring high-level standards for each observation.

I hold a MScE degree from the University of Chile. My thesis was related to the design and development an Out-of-Equilibrium Experiment for the Cubesat platform. This type of experiment was the first of its kind and was carried by the Satellite of Univeristy of Chile for Aerospace Investigation (SUCHAI), which was also the first satellite of the University of Chile.

During my master and undergraduate studies I have worked as a Teacher Assistant in several courses of Electrical Engineering, Physics and Computer Sciences.

Finally, in my free time I enjoy playing sports and doing astrophotography with a amateur telescope.
